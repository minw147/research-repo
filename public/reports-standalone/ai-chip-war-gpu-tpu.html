Actionable Insights for AI Investors

  1. Roofline matters at scale – Communication bottlenecks (memory, networking) often limit training before raw compute. Invest in companies addressing interconnect and memory bandwidth, not only peak FLOPS.
  2. Tensor-core roadmaps are key – Vendors with sustained tensor-core doubling (V100 → B200) are structurally aligned with LLM workloads.
  3. GPU vs TPU – Both pursue the same core idea. The competition is in ecosystem, deployment, and total cost of ownership.
