Usability Study: Checkout Flow

Key findings regarding the new payment gateway integration, focusing on user confusion during method selection.

Updated 2 days ago High Priority

1. Payment Method Visibility

During the sessions, several participants noted confusion when selecting a payment method. The placement of the credit card icons was often missed because it was positioned below the fold on smaller laptop screens.

play_arrow
00:45
Participant 4 04:12

"I kept looking for the Visa logo on the left, but it was hidden under the dropdown. I honestly didn't think I could pay with card at first."

sentiment_dissatisfied Confusion #checkout-flow

This sentiment was echoed by three other users in the same cohort. We recommend moving the payment logos to a more prominent position above the form fields, rather than nesting them within the selector component.
