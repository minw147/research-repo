Q3 Customer Usability Study: Checkout Flow Optimization

Dr. Sarah Jenkins
calendar_today Oct 12, 2023
schedule 8 min read
Checkout Mobile Conversion

lightbulb Executive Summary

Users are abandoning the checkout process primarily due to hidden shipping costs revealed only at the final step. Optimizing the "Review Order" screen to show estimated costs earlier could potentially increase conversion rates by 15-20%.

This study focused on the mobile checkout experience for new customers. We conducted moderated usability testing with 12 participants matching our core demographic. The primary goal was to identify friction points in the payment and address entry forms.

Key Pain Points

Critical 8/12 Users

Unexpected Shipping Costs

Users felt "tricked" when shipping was added at the very end. This was the #1 reason for cart abandonment during the test.

High Priority 5/12 Users

Guest Checkout Visibility

The "Continue as Guest" link is too small and placed below the fold on mobile devices, forcing users to attempt login.
