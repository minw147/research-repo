# Mobile Onboarding Flow

Summary of usability findings for the first-time user onboarding experience on the mobile app.

## 1. Sign-up Friction

Participants found the email verification step redundant when they had already connected via Google Sign-In.

<Clip
  src="/videos/demo-clip.mp4"
  label="I already signed in with Google—why am I typing my email again? It felt like the app forgot who I was."
  participant="Participant 2"
  duration="01:45"
  start={30}
/>

## Actionable Insight

Skip email verification when the user has authenticated via OAuth. Add a one-tap "Continue" to proceed directly to the home screen.
